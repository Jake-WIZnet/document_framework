iMCU

Ethernet MCU with Hardwired TCP/IP Core

Overview#

IOP is the Internet Offload Processor, the one-chip solution that integrates MCU and Hardwired TCP/IP cores.

Product Family#

  • W7500: ARM Cortex-M0, 128KB Flash, Hardwired TCP/IP, 802.3 Ethernet MAC
  • W7500P: ARM Cortex-M0, 128KB Flash, Hardwired TCP/IP, 802.3 Ethernet MAC w/ PHY
  • W7100A: 80c51 compatible core, 64KB Flash, Hardwired TCP/IP, MAC w/ PHY
